Soon

We started going outside

But the world was scary

Everything was so new and overwhelming!

Mom and I worked hard on my courage

But I was still afraid

But at home, I was brave!

One day, I dared to jump onto the couch

Then I jumped off…

and hurt my paw

Mom rushed me to the hospital. They did something to my paw, but walking was tough for a few more months.

Turns out, no one had cared for my tummy before—it often hurt from bad food. And my ears? They were itchy and sore.
